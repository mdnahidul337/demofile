Diploma in Computer Science Engineering

Program Objective

The Department of Computer Science and Technology, established in 2002, is a recognized center for academic excellence and research. With experienced and motivated faculty, the department focuses on combining theoretical instruction with hands-on application. The aim is to produce adaptable, innovative engineers who are eager problem solvers and active contributors to technological development.

Course Duration & Assessment

This is a four-year diploma program designed to prepare mid-level managers and technical supervisors. Students can enroll after passing SSC. The program is governed by the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB).

Career Opportunities

This diploma opens the door to careers in government, private sectors, IT, power plants, and beyond. With a job-focused curriculum, students are equipped to enter the workforce directly or pursue higher studies in related fields.

Eligibility for Admission

  • Minimum GPA of 2.00 in SSC or equivalent
  • Science HSC students: Direct entry into 3rd semester
  • Vocational HSC students: Direct entry into 4th semester

Admission Requirements

  • 3 passport-size photos
  • Original SSC transcript
  • Completed admission form
