How It Works

Your Complete Guide to Using Our Student Management System

Step 1: Create Your Account

Simply register with your student ID and email. Once verified, you'll have access to your personal dashboard.

Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Add your personal information, upload your photo, and select your department. This helps us personalize your experience.

Step 3: Access Your Information

View your courses, check your grades, download documents, and track your academic progress all in one place.

What Can You Do With This System?

For Students

  • View and download your semester results
  • Check your attendance records
  • Access course materials
  • Update your personal information
  • Track your academic progress

For Parents

  • Monitor your child's progress
  • View attendance records
  • Check semester results
  • Stay updated with notifications
  • Communicate with teachers
